Pancakes Recipe

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite—soft, fluffy, and easy to make. They are cooked on a flat pan and can be served with syrup, butter, fruits, or even chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth (do not overmix).
  3. Heat the Pan: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  4. Cook the Pancake: Pour batter onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip.
  5. Finish Cooking: Cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve: Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Additional Info

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
